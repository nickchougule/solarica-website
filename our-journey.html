THE PATH WE
FORGED

From a small R&D lab to a national leader in solar innovation. Explore the milestones that defined our legacy.

2001

The Inception

Solarica Energy was founded with a singular vision: to harness the sun's immense power. We began in a small R&D lab, laying the groundwork for future renewable technologies.

2002

First Prototype

After intense research, our team successfully developed our first high-efficiency photovoltaic cell prototype, achieving conversion rates previously unseen in domestic markets.

2003

Manufacturing Begins

We secured seed funding to establish our first pilot manufacturing facility in Pune. The transition from lab bench to production line officially began.

2004

First Deployment

A milestone year. Solarica successfully powered its first commercial complex, proving the viability and reliability of our technology in real-world scenarios.

2005

R&D Breakthrough

Our R&D wing achieved a significant breakthrough in reducing silicon wastage during production, making our panels more cost-effective and eco-friendly.

2006

National Expansion

We expanded operations beyond Maharashtra, setting up regional offices and partnering with distributors across five major Indian states.

2007

Innovation Award

Solarica received the prestigious "National Energy Innovation Award" for our contribution to sustainable manufacturing practices and green technology.

2008

The Mega Project

We were commissioned for our first 10MW utility-scale solar plant project, solidifying our position as a major player in the Indian energy sector.
